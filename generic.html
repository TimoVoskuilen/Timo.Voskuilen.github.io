Open
science

Open science is a movement to make scientific research accessible to all levels of society, amateur or professional. A key term in the open science community is reproducible research, which means that research data and code is made available so that others are able to reach the same result as claimed in scientific literature, and hopefully be able to reach similar conclusions. A key focus in my education has been on the replicability principle in order to increase the quality of my research output. the concept of Reproducibility is rather easy to understand, following five steps: Fomulating a hypothesis, Designing the study, Running the study and collecting data, analyzing data, reporting the study. All these steps need to be clearly reported and and open documentation must be provided, making the study transparant and reproducible.
